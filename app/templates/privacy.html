1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how CityGuide AI collects, uses, discloses, stores, and protects information when you use our website, account features, local discovery tools, and AI-powered chat service.

Our goal is to collect only the information reasonably needed to provide and secure the service. The FTC recommends that businesses understand what data they hold, minimize unnecessary collection, protect retained information, and dispose of it appropriately. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

2. Information you provide

We may collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Your name and email address.
  • Your password in securely hashed form.
  • Google account information when you choose Google sign-in.
  • Search queries and messages sent through the AI chat.
  • Saved places, collections, preferences, and feedback.
  • Messages sent to customer support.

We will not store your plain-text password.

3. Information collected automatically

When you use CityGuide AI, we may automatically receive limited technical information such as:

  • Browser and device type.
  • IP address.
  • Operating system.
  • Pages viewed and feature interactions.
  • Approximate location derived from your IP address.
  • Security, error, and diagnostic logs.

4. Location information

CityGuide AI may request access to your device location to provide nearby recommendations. Precise location access should occur only after you grant browser or device permission.

You may deny or revoke location permission through your browser or device settings. You may also enter a city or location manually.

5. How we use information

We may use information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Authenticate users and maintain secure sessions.
  • Respond to local discovery requests.
  • Personalize recommendations with user permission.
  • Save conversations, places, and collections.
  • Operate, test, troubleshoot, and improve the service.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • Communicate service and security updates.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

6. AI processing

Chat messages and relevant context may be sent to an AI service provider to generate responses. Before launch, this section must name the provider, describe applicable retention settings, and explain whether submitted content may be used to train provider models.

Do not submit highly sensitive information such as passwords, government identification numbers, financial account numbers, medical records, or confidential legal information.

7. Google sign-in

If you choose Google sign-in, Google may provide CityGuide AI with account information such as your verified email address, name, profile image, and Google account identifier.

Google processes information according to its own privacy practices. CityGuide AI should request only the permissions necessary for authentication.

8. Cookies and sessions

CityGuide AI may use cookies or similar technologies to maintain authenticated sessions, remember user settings, secure the service, and understand basic usage.

Authentication cookies should be configured with appropriate security protections, including HTTP-only, secure, and SameSite settings where applicable.

9. How information may be shared

We may share information:

  • With vendors that help host, secure, authenticate, analyze, or operate CityGuide AI.
  • With AI providers when necessary to respond to your requests.
  • When required by law, legal process, or a valid government request.
  • To protect users, CityGuide AI, or others from fraud, abuse, or security threats.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or transfer of the business, subject to appropriate safeguards.

CityGuide AI does not currently sell personal information. This statement must be updated if the business model or advertising practices change.

10. Advertising and sponsored results

CityGuide AI may display sponsored listings or advertisements. Sponsored placement will be labeled.

We will not use private chat content for targeted advertising unless we clearly disclose that practice and obtain any consent required by law.

We intend to retain personal information only for as long as needed to provide the service, protect its security, meet legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Before production launch, CityGuide AI must define specific retention periods for account records, chat history, security logs, deleted accounts, and backups.

We intend to use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the information we maintain. No system can guarantee absolute security.
